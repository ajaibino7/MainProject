Welcome to

VoxSum



Watching lengthy videos can often become a daunting task, particularly when individuals are in search of specific information or concise summaries. This not only hampers the efficiency of learning but also leads to frustration among users who prefer streamlined content consumption experiences. Additionally, the lack of interactivity on traditional video platforms confines users to passive viewing, depriving them of opportunities for clarification, elaboration, or addressing queries that may arise during the video playback. This issue is further compounded for individuals with hearing impairments, as audio-centric content like podcasts and YouTube videos poses significant accessibility challenges. Recognizing these obstacles, VoxSum aims to champion inclusivity by leveraging technology to convert audio content into text, thus widening the reach of online video content to a more diverse audience. By providing a comprehensive solution that facilitates the efficient consumption of video content, VoxSum endeavors to bridge the accessibility gap through audio-to-text conversion, concise summarization, and interactive features such as question-and-answer sessions. Through these initiatives, VoxSum seeks to empower users to engage fully with video content while addressing accessibility concerns and enhancing the overall viewing experience for all individuals, regardless of their hearing abilities or preferences for content consumption.
